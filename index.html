Educational YouTube Channels

Behind the YouTube algorithm recommending your interests, there are a lot of educational YouTube channels that can widen and deepen your knowledge.

1. Real Engineering

Real Engineering is a channel that discuss about wide variety of engineering such as aerospace engineering, mechanical engineering, civil engineering, and electrical engineering.

This channel provides in-depth explanations that easy to understand even when the topic is very complex and needs a lot of formulas.

The composition won't gives the old-school plain book impression, you'll like it like a story.

2. Real Science

Like Real Engineering, Real Science brings up broad topics of science including biology, physics, chemistry, and astronomy, such as rainforest, DNA, microorganism, earth environments, the biology of various animals and species, and many more.

This channel along with Real Engineering is an excellent educational channels that gives valuable insights you probably wouldn't expect anywhere else

3. The Slow Mo Guys

The Slow Mo Guys is a channel that specializes in creating slow-motion videos of various experiments and events using high-speed cameras to capture the footage of the experiments such as comparing the speed of cracking glass with a bullet, exploding spray paint, daytime fireworks, and many more!

This channel is great to see the world from different perspectives by slowing down the time (slow motion videos).

Not only that, they also produces educational videos such as how camera works, how monitor produces the display images that we can see, how sound travels, and the physics behind various sports that maybe most of us have probably never thought about

4. SmarterEveryDay

SmarterEveryDay is a channel about explorations that covers wide range of topics from physics, chemistry, even engineering that are well-prepared and well-researched to gives you complete understanding very engagingly.

The unique aspect of SmarterEveryDay is that many of its videos focused on understanding on the mechanics and physics behind everyday objects and events.

Final Thought

While there are many things to do and many content to watch, by finding and choosing the right one, we can find the most suitable activities we can do that gives us not only entertainment but we also can gain more and more values
